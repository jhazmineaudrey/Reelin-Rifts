# Reelin-Rifts - Pomodoro Timer
A simple game and productivity helper solely developed by Jhaz, where you fulfill your cat's food requests by fishing in different dimensions — while also getting in some productive time in!

## Reason for Development
I initially had the idea of a fishing game for a game jam, but due to personal matters I couldn't participate in it. But I really loved the idea of making a cozy fishing game since I've been meaning to develop a calmer game rather than a more tension/action-based game, and I decided to also add a productivity element in it through the form of a pomodoro timer since I thought it would be interesting.

Once the game is installed to your computer, you can:
- Set up your preferred settings for the pomodoro timer
- Start being productive — which gets you energy to fish
- Catch some fish in the different dimensions you have unlocked (Press 'E' to fish when the cursor on the fishbars land on the correct area)
- Fulfill your cat's requests by dragging and dropping the stated fish
- Earn points to unlock more dimensions and fishes which give you more points
- Check back in everyday to gain points and see how productive you've been at the end of the week!
